Makeup as a Form of Creative Expression

I've been experimenting with makeup since high school but really dove into it more during the pandemic. It's my favorite hobby and serves as both an outlet and a way to express my creativity. The pictures above show some close-up shots of looks I've done in the past. You might notice I look a bit more tired in some of them, that's probably because I had a lot of homework to get through, and doing my makeup was a fun way to distract myself for a bit. If I really like a look I make on the spot, I'll take a photo and recreate with more detail later. I'm especially into glitter eyeshadows, graphic liner, and experimenting with rhinestones to take my makeup to the next level.
